Removals

Native Removal Act

1830-

Under President Andrew Jackson, the Indian Removal Act empowers the U.S. Government with the policy and funds used to “persuade, bribe, and threaten” Indigenous peoples into land-exchange treaties (“removals”) for lands assigned to them in “Indian Territory” west of the Mississippi.
